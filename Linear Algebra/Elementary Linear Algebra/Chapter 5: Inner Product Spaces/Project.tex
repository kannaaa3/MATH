    \section{ \textcolor{blue5}{The \textit{QR}-Factorization} }
    
    The Gram-Schmidt orthonormalization process leads to an important factorization of matrices called the 
    \textbf{ \textit{QR}-factorization}. If $A$ is an $m \x n$ matrix of rank $n$, then $A$ can be expressed as the product $A = QR$
    of an $m \x n$ matrix $Q$ and an $n \x n$ matrix $R$, where $Q$ has orthonormal columns and $R$ is upper triangular.
